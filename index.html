About

Editing for character, structure, and emotional precision.

Zimo Huang is an Emmy-nominated editor born in Beijing, raised in the United States, and based in Los Angeles. His work spans documentary features, scripted drama, sci-fi horror, docuseries, travel programming, and commercial campaigns.

Career highlights include the Academy Award-nominated documentary feature Glen Campbell: I'll Be Me, Sundance feature The Tomorrow Man, Netflix series Live to 100: Secrets of the Blue Zones, and a Best Editing award for Days and Nights in Wuhan.
